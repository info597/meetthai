Meet Thai – Safety Standards

At Meet Thai, we are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users.

Zero Tolerance Policy

We have a strict zero-tolerance policy for any form of sexual abuse and exploitation of children (CSAE). Any such content or behavior will result in immediate account suspension and reporting to relevant authorities.

User Safety

Users can report inappropriate behavior, content, or other users directly within the app. All reports are reviewed and acted upon promptly.

Moderation

We actively monitor and moderate user-generated content to ensure compliance with our policies. Violations may result in content removal or permanent account bans.

Compliance

We comply with all applicable laws and cooperate with law enforcement agencies when required.
